A safe place to heal, grow, and thrive.

Our adult residential homes provide safe, structured support for people aged 18 and above, including individuals with mental health needs, substance misuse histories, and forensic backgrounds.

Every resident is treated with empathy and respect, supported equally, and encouraged to grow in confidence and independence. Our compassionate team is available 24/7, creating homely environments where people can recover and rebuild their lives.

The Homes

These are the adult's residential homes at Specialist Care Limited

Little Acorn

A welcoming 15-bed coastal home with en-suite bedrooms, communal spaces, and a secure garden.

  • Secure and supportive environment
  • Focus on healing, growth, and recovery
  • Emphasis on empowerment

High Road House

A spacious 5-bedroom farmhouse with a homely, open, and large secure garden.

  • Trauma-informed counseling and therapy
  • Behavioral and emotional support services
  • One-on-one and group sessions with licensed therapists

Earles Garden House

A modern 6-bedroom home with accessibility features including a stair lift and wet room.

  • Homework help and tutoring
  • School enrollment assistance and advocacy
  • Learning assessments and individualized education plans (IEPs)

Homesdale House

A warm and inviting 4-bedroom home with bright bedrooms, communal spaces, and a safe outdoor area.

  • Financial literacy and budgeting workshops
  • Cooking, cleaning, and home management
  • Career guidance and mentorship programs
