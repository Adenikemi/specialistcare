About Us

We are proud to deliver specialist residential care supporting people with various needs to help them lead the highest quality of life. We are focused on providing person-centred support, which we achieve by asking about and understanding people’s wants, needs, dreams and aspirations.

Our Strategic Direction

Specialist care's strategic direction focuses on delivering personalized, high-quality support tailored to the unique needs of individuals with complex health or social care requirements.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to help people improve their wellbeing and reach their potential through the provision of high quality, compassionate and responsive residential care.

Our Approach

Individualized Support Plans

By working to an individual plan of support that has been agreed with the service user, we promote and increase their independence.

Positive Behaviour Support

Through PBS, we empower individuals and improve lives by working collaboratively with families and continuously developing our skilled staff.

Community Access

We support service users to access all community services available to them, ensuring they have trained and competent staff providing support.

Promoting Independence

We work with each service user to promote and, where possible, increase their independence. Our person-centered approach focuses on building life skills and confidence, empowering individuals to take an active role in their daily lives.

Trained and Competent Staff

We ensure that all support is provided by trained and competent staff. Our team members undergo continuous professional development to stay current with best practices and specialized support techniques.
